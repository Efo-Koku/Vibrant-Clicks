About Me

My name is Phillimon. I am a paid part-time photographer based in London. I developed an interest in photography from an early age which resulted in me taking pictures of anything that inspired me. When I bought my first professional camera, I took it upon myself to travel so I could take photos of anything of interest; buildings, roads, wildlife, motor vehicles etc. I later delved into other forms of photohraphy and when I started taking portraits of people, something clicked in my head and I knew I had found my calling...

I love working directly with models; orchestrating photo shoots, directing and positioning people and objects to capture moments that is invisible to the untrained eye. My goal is to show the beauty, grace, strength and enthusiasm of people. I don’t always work with models, I also find joy in working with regular people in exaggerated settings that highlight an aspect of who they are. I look for a sense of defiance and fortitude in my work.
